Description

This is a quick and easypan-fried chicken recipe using chicken breasts with a simple pan sauce made with capers, butter, white wine, and lemon juice.

Per serving nutritional facts follows:321 calories; protein 24.7g; carbohydrates 8.4g; fat 18.2g; cholesterol 87.5mg; sodium 223.6mg. Full Nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breat halves
  • cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • all-purpose flour for dredging
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon capers, drained
  • 1/2 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons cold unsalted butter, cut in 1/4-inch slices
  • 2 tablespoons fresh Italian parsley, chopped
